FAQ

Here are some of our most asked questions.

What API's do you use?

We use the BreweryDB API to get information on local breweries near you, then we use Google Map's own API in order to display that information on a map with directions.

How accurate is the site?

We use the same technology that Google does, so we can guarentee pin point accuracy on all of the maps and directions on our site.

How do you reccomend breweries?

When you enter your location, we pull from a massive database of breweries from around the world. We find the best places that are closest to your geographic location.

How many breweries can I add to my trip?

You can add an unlimited amount of breweries to your itinerary. Keeping that in mind, please drink responsiblity.

What if my brewery isn't on the list?

We are always trying to offer more to our users. Please email us at info@brewventures.com and we will work on adding your brewery to the database that we use.

What are your plans for the future?

Our team plans on expanding our feature set through the use of innovation and new web technologies. In the near future we hope to build our own BrewVentures iPhone and Andriod app.
