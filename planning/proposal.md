# Team Project 1

**Title:** BrewVenture  
**Team Members:** Tyler Olli, Jarrett Tolman, Rayal, Hannah King  
**Description:** Plan your own local Brew Hop!  
**APIs:** Google Maps, breweryDB, untappd, routeXL  

## Goals:
  1. User friendly
  2. Social sharing
  3. Search previous trips by user, trip name, date, location
